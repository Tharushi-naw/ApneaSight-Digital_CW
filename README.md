# ApneaSight

ApneaSight is a digital health microsite created to present a concept focused on sleep apnea awareness, screening support, and intelligent healthcare technology.

## Overview

The microsite was designed to communicate the project clearly and professionally, highlighting the importance of early awareness and better digital support for sleep-related health conditions.

## Live Microsite

Visit the live site here:

[ApneaSight Microsite](https://sites.google.com/iit.ac.lk/apneasight?usp=sharing)

## Purpose

ApneaSight aims to:

- Raise awareness about sleep apnea
- Present a clean and informative digital health experience
- Demonstrate a healthcare-focused web concept
- Support project presentation and online visibility
